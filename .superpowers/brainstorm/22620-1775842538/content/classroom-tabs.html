Inside a classroom — which tab arrangement?

Google Classroom uses 4 tabs. EMIS has more concepts (attendance, exams, continuous assessment, results) so a strict mirror doesn't quite fit. Pick the structure you want.

Faithful GC — 4 tabs, "Classwork" rolls everything up
