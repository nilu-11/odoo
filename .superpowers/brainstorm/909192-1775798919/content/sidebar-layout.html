Which sidebar collapse style works best?

All three are collapsible with a hamburger toggle. They differ in how the sidebar behaves when collapsed.

≡
EMIS Portal

A. Icon Rail (Collapsed)

When collapsed, sidebar shrinks to a narrow icon rail — icons remain visible. Clicking hamburger expands it back to show labels. Best for power users — always-on navigation without taking much space.

≡
EMIS Portal

B. Fully Hidden (Off-canvas)

When collapsed, sidebar is completely hidden. Clicking hamburger slides it back in as an overlay. Best for content focus — maximum reading space when you don't need the menu.

≡
EMIS Portal
Dashboard

C. Icon Rail with Hover Tooltips

Same as A but when collapsed, hovering over an icon shows a tooltip with the label and sub-items. Best of both worlds — maximum space + quick navigation.
