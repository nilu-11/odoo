Top row of KPI cards (classrooms, students, pending tasks), quick action buttons, then two columns of today's schedule and recent activity feed. Action-oriented — helps teachers jump straight to what needs doing.

B. Next-Action Hero Card

A large hero card at the top showing the user's next immediate action (next class to mark attendance, next marks deadline). Below it, a 2x2 grid of context cards. Context-oriented — tells you what to focus on right now.
